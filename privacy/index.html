Privacy Policy

How MogUp handles your information.

This policy explains what MogUp collects, how that information is used to generate scan results and personalized protocol guidance, and how the app handles subscription and progress data.

Last updated: April 6, 2026

What MogUp collects

MogUp may collect the photo you choose to upload for analysis, the answers you provide during onboarding, scan results generated inside the app, protocol progress and streak activity, and subscription or entitlement status needed to unlock premium features.

How your information is used

Your information is used to generate facial analysis results, personalize your recommended protocol, measure progress across rescans, restore your subscription entitlements, and improve reliability and product performance.

Third-party services

MogUp may rely on Apple services for payments and platform features, and RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management. GitHub Pages may host these legal pages, but it does not host your uploaded scans, progress history, or in-app personal data.

Data retention

Saved scan history and related progress information may remain available inside the app until you remove the app, clear your saved data, or the product changes its retention policy. Subscription records are also subject to Apple and RevenueCat data handling practices.

Children

MogUp is not intended for young children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app, contact the publisher using the support method listed on the app’s product page.

Changes

This policy may be updated over time to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The latest version should always be published at this URL.

This page is a practical starter policy for publishing with GitHub Pages. Review it and adapt it to your final support contact, retention practices, analytics stack, and jurisdiction before launch.
