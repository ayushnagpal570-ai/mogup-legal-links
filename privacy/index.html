Privacy Policy

How MogUp handles your information.

This policy explains what information MogUp uses, how it is used to generate scan results and personalized protocol guidance, and how subscription and progress data are handled.

Last updated: April 6, 2026

What MogUp collects

MogUp uses the photo you choose for analysis, the answers you provide during onboarding, scan results generated inside the app, protocol progress and streak activity, and subscription or entitlement status needed to unlock premium features.

How your information is used

Your information is used to generate facial analysis results, personalize your recommended protocol, measure progress across rescans, and restore your subscription entitlements.

On-device and local storage

MogUp is designed so your scan-related data stays on your device and is stored locally for your progress history. MogUp’s public legal pages are hosted separately and do not store your scan photos or in-app progress records.

Third-party services

MogUp relies on Apple services for payments and platform features, and RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management. GitHub Pages hosts these legal pages only. It does not host your uploaded scans, progress history, or in-app personal data.

Data retention

Saved scan history and related progress information may remain available inside the app until you remove the app or clear your local app data. Subscription records are also subject to Apple and RevenueCat data handling practices.

Children

MogUp is not intended for young children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app, contact us at ayushnagpal570@icloud.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated over time to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The latest version should always be published at this URL.
