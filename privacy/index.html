Privacy Policy

How MogUp handles your information.

This policy explains what information MogUp uses, including face photos and on-device facial measurements, how it is used to generate scan results and personalized protocol guidance, and how subscription and progress data are handled.

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What MogUp collects

MogUp uses the photo you choose for analysis, face landmarks and blendshape-style facial measurements generated on-device from that photo during the scan flow, the answers you provide during onboarding, scan results generated inside the app, protocol progress and streak activity, and subscription or entitlement status needed to unlock premium features.

Face data

For MogUp, face data means the user-selected face photo and the facial landmark and blendshape-style measurements derived from that photo during analysis. MogUp does not use Face ID, TrueDepth identity verification, or any feature intended to determine who you are.

How your information is used

Your information is used to generate facial analysis results and scan overlays, personalize your recommended protocol, measure progress across rescans, and restore your subscription entitlements.

On-device and local storage

MogUp is designed so scan-related data stays on your device. The selected photo and derived facial landmark and blendshape-style measurements are processed on-device during the scan flow, and saved scan results, questionnaire answers, and progress history are stored locally on the device. MogUp’s public legal pages are hosted separately and do not store your scan photos or in-app progress records.

Third-party services

MogUp relies on Apple services for payments and platform features, and RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management. MogUp does not share raw face photos, facial landmark data, blendshape-style measurements, or saved scan results with those third parties. GitHub Pages hosts these legal pages only and does not host your uploaded scans, progress history, or in-app personal data.

Data retention

The raw photo and derived facial landmark and blendshape-style measurements are used during the active scan flow and are not kept by MogUp as separate long-term records. MogUp may retain the latest journey plus up to 12 saved local scan-history entries, which contain questionnaire answers and derived scan results, until you remove the app or clear local app data. Subscription records are also subject to Apple and RevenueCat data handling practices.

Children

MogUp is not intended for young children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app, contact us at ayushnagpal570@icloud.com.
