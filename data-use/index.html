Data Use

What MogUp uses your data for.

This is the shorter, plain-language version of how MogUp uses the information you provide inside the app.

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Your face data

Your selected photo is analyzed on-device to detect facial landmarks and blendshape-style measurements. MogUp uses those inputs to generate scan results, score breakdowns, and the results card shown in the app.

Where it is stored

MogUp is designed to keep raw photos and scan-related analysis on your device. MogUp does not send raw face photos or derived landmark and blendshape-style measurements to third parties.

Your onboarding answers

Your answers help tailor the protocol, focus areas, and pacing of your personalized plan.

Your progress history

Saved scan results, streak activity, and completed tasks help MogUp show changes over time and maintain continuity across your current protocol.

Your subscription status

Subscription status is used to determine whether premium features such as full analysis results and protocol access should be unlocked.

How long it is retained

The raw photo and derived landmark and blendshape-style measurements are used during the active scan flow and are not kept as separate long-term records. Saved scan results and answers may remain locally on the device for history, including the latest journey and up to 12 saved scans, until the app is deleted or its local data is cleared.

GitHub Pages only serves these legal pages publicly. It does not store the in-app photo, scan, or progress data used by MogUp itself.
